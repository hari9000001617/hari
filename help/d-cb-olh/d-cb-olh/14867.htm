Convergent Billing Overview

Convergent Billing is designed for new generation customer care and billing environments to increase competitive advantage by making it easier to design, deliver, and bill products and services required by customers.

Billing environments are rapidly changing to support complex products and product packaging, consolidated billing (single bill), rapid tariff definition, integrated customer care systems, and billing support for targeted services.

Sophisticated billing is a key factor in customer retention and acquisition of new customers.

Convergent Billing provides modules for balance management, billing mediation, rating, discounting, and bill production.

Convergent Billing is based on industry standard architecture, relational database technology, and interfaces, to ensure portability across operating platforms.

The Convergent Billing Solution

Convergent Billing offers a competitive advantage by making it easier to design, deliver, and bill the products and services required by customers.

The core technology is the high performance, highly flexible rating and discounting engine, used for construction of arbitrary tariff and discount rules based on customer data, and call and other events.

Convergent Billing offers unrivalled support for:

  • Flexible tariffing, discount, and product packaging that allow tailoring of solutions to customers' requirements.
  • Rapid configuration of new products and tariffs.
  • Consolidated (single) billing so that customers view their total costs at a glance.
  • Real-time customer balance management (only available as part of the separate Singleview Commerce Engine licence option).
  • Online access to customer account, product, and tariff information for instant response to customer enquiries.
  • Financial reporting including accounts receivable sub-ledger, Sales and Cash Receipts Journal, and automated upload to external general ledger systems.
  • Comprehensive security features for controlling access to information and use of Convergent Billing, including extended authentication with password expiry, password reuse, account locking and the use of third party authentication systems.
  • High availability of the Commerce Engine and Online Transaction Processing (OLTP), such as user transactions.
  • Multi-server billing and OLTP scalability.
  • Continuous availability of the Commerce Engine.
  • Tracking and monitoring Convergent Billing system components.

Convergent Billing provides the features and facilities required to change the concept of billing and customer service from a costly, labour-intensive, and repetitive function to a streamlined, automated business process suitable for all service providers.
