Statements

Statements provide a record of the charges billed to a customer and are provided only for the customer’s information.

The following can be viewed:

  • Details of each statement generated for a customer, including statement number, account number, total amount, and details of each transaction.
  • Electronic copy (image) of the printed statement sent to the customer.
  • Image of the statement immediately after it is generated.

Statements are generated only if the report level of the customer node is set to Statement. Any charges resulting from the use of services at a customer node where the report level is Statement must be billed to an account on a customer node where the report level is set to Invoice. The flexibility of Convergent Billing allows this invoice account to be an account on a node in the same customer hierarchy or on a node in a different hierarchy.

The appearance of a statement, level of detail it contains, and frequency at which it is generated are specified for a customer when that customer’s details are recorded in Convergent Billing.

The frequency at which a customer is sent a statement is determined by the required invoice cycle. The level of detail displayed on the statement and its layout are determined by the required invoice format.

When generated, each statement is assigned a statement number by which it can be referenced.
