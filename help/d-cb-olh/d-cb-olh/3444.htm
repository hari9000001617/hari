Person Detail Form

The Person Detail form is used to enter, view, and update a contact’s details.

Person, position, or organisation recorded in Singleview that may need to be contacted. A contact can have multiple accounts, addresses, or other details.

Context: Convergent Billing, Customer Management, Process Engine

See also Customer, Customer Contact, Primary Contact, and Secondary Contact.

The upper part of the form is used to enter personal information about the contact such as their name, gender, type, status, birth date, and the date range over which the record for that contact is to be effective.

Clicking the:

  • Changes button displays the Summary of Change form with changes made between the current record and the previous record.
  • Queries button displays the Customer Query List form, for viewing queries associated with the person record.
  • References button displays the Person Reference form with details of records (customers, payments, queries, and so on) that have this person assigned to them.
