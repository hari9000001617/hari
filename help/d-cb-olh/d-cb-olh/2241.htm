Contract Detail Form

The Contract Detail form is used to enter, view, update, and cancel contract details.

Legal agreement between the organisation and the customer to apply special terms and conditions to products and services for a specific duration.

Context: Convergent Billing

The upper part of the form displays, or allows entry of, basic details about the contract (for example, contract type, customer reference number, and contract status).

The lower part of the form consists of the following tabs that allow more specific details to be displayed or entered:

Dates

The Dates tab specifies:

  • Details about the duration of the contract (date-time the contract commences, length of the contract, date-time the contract is expected to expire, date-time the contract expires, and any grace period)
  • Date-time the account manager is notified that the contract needs to be renegotiated or that the contract is about to expire.

Customer

The Customer tab specifies details about the customer who has entered into the contract (for example, name, type of customer, account number, and status of their account).

Contact

The Contact tab specifies details about the primary contact for the contract. The information on this tab is entered automatically based on the customer information entered, but can also be modified.

Account Management

The Account Management tab specifies the method by which the contract was obtained (the sales channel), user responsible for the contract record, and account manager responsible for the contract.

Overridden Contracts

The Overridden Contracts tab specifies any contracts that are superseded by this contract.

Related Documents

The Related Documents tab specifies any documents related to the contract.

Comments

The Comments tab specifies any general purpose comments relevant to the contract.

General

The General tab specifies additional information about the customer.

Note:

The fields displayed on this tab depend on the customer type specified.

The form can be displayed in one of three form modes, with the current mode indicated in the form’s title.

A form can be displayed in one of the following modes:

Associated Actions

Entering Contract Details

To create a contract record:

  1. Display the appropriate customer on the Customer Hierarchy form.
  2. Drag the Contract icon onto the appropriate customer node.
  3. Click the Contract icon and, while holding down the left mouse button, move the cursor until the required customer's name is highlighted. Drop the icon by releasing the mouse button. The Contract Detail form is displayed in Insert mode.

    Note:

    As the icon is dragged onto the left-hand panel of the Customer Hierarchy form, the cursor changes from one depicting a stop symbol to one with an arrow. If the icon is dropped when the stop symbol is displayed, no action is taken.

  4. Enter the contract details.
  5. To allocate all compatible services in the customer's hierarchy to the contract, ensure that the Allocate all services? check box is selected.
  6. If the Allocate all services? check box is selected, in addition to all available services being added to the contract, any new services created for the customer are also automatically added to the contract.
  7. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Updating Contract Details

To update a contract record:

  1. View the appropriate contract.
  2. Click the Update button on the toolbar. The Contract Detail form is displayed in Update mode.
  3. Update the contract details as required.
  4. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Need more information on contracts?

Viewing Contract Details

  1. To view contract details:
  2. Select the Customer Care Contracts Manage Contracts option. The Contract Search form is displayed.
  3. Enter or select the appropriate search criteria.
  4. Click the Search… button.

    If the search returns:

    • A single contract, the Contract Detail form is displayed in View Only mode (if the Always display list? option is not selected).
    • Multiple contracts, or the Always display list? option is selected, the Contract List form is displayed.
  5. To access the Contract Detail form from the Contract List form, double-click the appropriate contract record in the list of retrieved contracts, or select the appropriate record and click the Details… button.

Allocating a Service to a Contract

To allocate a service to a contract:

  1. View the appropriate contract.
  2. Click the Service Search… button. The Service Search form is displayed.
  3. Enter the appropriate search criteria and ensure that the Available Services option is selected.
  4. Click the Search… button. The Available Services List form is displayed in view only mode.
  5. Click the Update button on the toolbar.
  6. Select the appropriate service and click the Allocate button. A contract icon is displayed next to the selected service.
  7. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Need more information on contracts?

Removing a Service from a Contract

To remove a service from a contract:

  1. View the appropriate contract.
  2. Click the Service Search… button. The Service Search form is displayed.
  3. Enter the appropriate search criteria and ensure that the Allocated Services option is selected.
  4. Click the Search… button. The Allocated Service List form is displayed in View Only mode.
  5. Click the Update button on the toolbar.
  6. Select the appropriate service and click the Deallocate button.
  7. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Need more information about contracts?

Cancelling Contract Details

To cancel a contract:

  1. View the appropriate contract.
  2. Click the Delete button on the toolbar. A Confirmation window is displayed.
  3. Set the date-time from when the cancellation is to take effect. The cancellation date must be within the date range of the current record. If today’s date is within the date range of the current record, the cancellation date defaults to today’s date. Otherwise, the cancellation date defaults to the Start Date of the record.
  4. Click the Continue Cancellation button. The message 'Record cancelled successfully' is displayed.
  5. Click the OK button to confirm that the contract be cancelled.

Need more information on contracts?

Deleting a Contract

A contract can only be deleted if it has a status of 'Cancelled', or if it has no services associated with it. To delete a contract:

  1. View the appropriate contract.
  2. Click the History button on the toolbar. The Contract History form is displayed.
  3. Click the Delete button on the toolbar.
  4. Click the Save button on the toolbar.
