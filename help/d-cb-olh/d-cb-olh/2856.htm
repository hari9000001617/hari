Equipment Detail Form

The Equipment Detail form is used to enter, view, and update equipment details.

Physical components required to provide a service; for example, modem, leased line, or mobile phone. Equipment information is stored for information purposes only, but can also be used to allocate service numbers.

Context: Convergent Billing, Customer Management

See also Service.

The upper part of the form is used to enter or view general information about the item of equipment such as type, serial number(s), and status.

The lower part of the form consists of the following tabs for entering specific details about the equipment (some of these tabs are not always displayed, depending on the configuration of the selected equipment type):

Details

The Details tab is used to specify more explicit details for the selected equipment type.

The fields in the tab establish the make, model, origin, and network address of the equipment type. Facilities are also provided to specify a warranty.

Range

The Range tab displays the range information for the item of equipment including the number of equipment items within the range, To Serial Number (the last serial number in the range), and To Network Address.

The Range tab is only displayed if the selected equipment type supports ranges of equipment items.

Address

The Address tab is used to specify the address of the customer associated with the selected equipment item.

Supplier

The Supplier tab is used to specify supplier details associated with the selected equipment item.

Reservation

The Reservation tab is used to reserve the equipment item for a specific customer.

Warranty

The Warranty tab is used to define a warranty for the item of equipment.

Security

The Security tab displays specific security information such as the PIN number or PUK number for the item of equipment.

The tab is only displayed if the equipment type is configured to enable the tab.

General

The General tab is used to enter additional information about the equipment type.

Note:

The tab displays only those fields configured in the equipment type to be enabled.

Transit

The Transit tab displays details of the equipment while in transit. The tab is only displayed if the equipment status is set to In-Transit.

The form can be displayed in one of three form modes, with the current mode being indicated in the form’s title.

A form can be displayed in one of the following modes:

Associated Actions

Viewing Equipment Details

To view equipment details:

  1. Select the Customer Care Equipment Manage Equipment option. The Equipment Search form is displayed.

    Note:

    The Transit tab is only displayed on the Equipment Search form if the equipment type is configured to enable the tab.

  2. Enter or select the appropriate search criteria.
  3. Click the Search… button. A list of equipment records matching the specified criteria is displayed.
  4. Select the appropriate equipment record and click the Details... button. The Equipment Detail form is displayed in View Only mode.

Recording Equipment Details

Convergent Billing provides two methods in which equipment details can be recorded in the equipment inventory:

  • Individually
  • In bulk.

To record (enter) equipment details for individual items of equipment:

  1. Select either of the following options:
    • Customer Care Equipment New Equipment Items option.
    • Customer Care Equipment Manage Equipment option.

    The Equipment Search form is displayed.

  2. Select the Create new equipment option.
  3. Select the equipment type and enter the equipment serial number.
  4. Click the New… button to insert a new equipment record. The Equipment Detail form is displayed in Insert mode.
  5. Complete the appropriate data entry fields.
  6. Click the Save button.

To record (enter) equipment items in bulk:

  1. Create the appropriate equipment record.
  2. Click the Bulk Insert… button. The Equipment Bulk Insert form is displayed in Insert mode.
  3. Enter the serial number and/or network address for a specified item of equipment.
  4. To add a new item, press the Down Arrow key to create an empty line and repeat step 3.
  5. Repeat step 4 for each item of equipment to be inserted.
  6. Click the Save button on the toolbar.
  7. Close the Equipment Bulk Insert form to return control to the Equipment Detail form.

Updating Equipment Details

Convergent Billing provides facilities to update:

  • Individual equipment records
  • A range of two or more equipment records of the same equipment type.

To update the details of an individual equipment record:

  1. View the appropriate equipment record.
  2. Click the Update button on the toolbar. The Equipment Detail form is displayed in Update mode.
  3. Update the equipment details as required.
  4. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

To update a range of equipment records of the same equipment type:

  1. Search the appropriate equipment records and list them in the Equipment List form.
  2. Select each equipment record to be included in the range to be updated.
  3. For a contiguous number of equipment records, select the first record in the required range and do either of the following:
    • Hold down the Shift key, and select the last record in the required range.
    • Specify the number of records to be updated in the Select how many items? field, and click the Select button.
  4. To select a number of individual equipment records, hold down the Ctrl key and select each record.

    Note:

    When more than one record is selected, the Detail… button on the Equipment List form is replaced with the Bulk Update button.

  5. Click the Bulk Update button. The Equipment Detail form is displayed in Update mode with details of the first equipment record in the selected range.
  6. Make any required changes to the displayed record. Any changes made to this record are also made to each of the other records in the selected range.
  7. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Deleting Equipment Details

To delete equipment details:

  1. View the appropriate equipment record.
  2. Click the Delete button on the toolbar.
  3. When prompted, specify the date from which to delete the equipment. Depending on security settings, the date consists of a single component (date), or two components (date and time).
  4. When prompted, click the Yes button to confirm that the equipment details are to be deleted.

Splitting a Range of Equipment Items

Why split a range of equipment items?

A range of equipment items may need to be split. For example, if a range of equipment items includes the serial numbers 0530 to 0539, and equipment items with serial numbers 0532 and 0533 are required to be reserved for a customer, the range of equipment items can be split to create following three ranges; 0530 to 0531, 0532 to 0533, and 0534 to 0539.

The original range of equipment is split by creating a new range 0532 to 0533, which is then removed from the original range. The properties of the first and last range remain the same as the original range. The range containing serial numbers 0532 to 0533 can then be changed to include a reservation for the appropriate customer.

To split a range of equipment items:

  1. Select the Customer Care Equipment Manage Equipment option. The Equipment Search form is displayed.
  2. Specify the appropriate search criteria, ensuring that the Number of Items field is completed and contains a number greater than one.
  3. Ensure the Always Display List? check box is selected.
  4. Click the Search… button. A list of the equipment records that match the specified criteria is displayed.
  5. Select the range of equipment to be split and click the Split… button. The Equipment Split form is displayed.
  6. Enter the appropriate information and click the Continue Split button. The Equipment Detail form is displayed containing information about the new range of equipment items.
  7. Click the Update button on the toolbar. The Equipment Detail form is displayed in Update mode.
  8. Update the form as required.
  9. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Reallocating Equipment

To reallocate equipment:

  1. Verify that the equipment to be allocated is recorded in the equipment inventory.
  2. View the appropriate product instance.
  3. Click the Update button on the toolbar. The Product Instance Detail form is displayed in Update mode.
  4. Select one of the equipment entries and click the Allocate button. The Equipment Allocation form is displayed.
  5. Select one of the following:
    • An item in each of the available equipment pools, and click the Select button. A list of valid equipment items is displayed. If no equipment pools are display in the Equipment Pool Select group, all equipment items held in the equipment inventory are listed.
    • Customer's equipment option and click the Select button. A list of equipment allocated to the specified customer is displayed.
  6. Choose one of the selection options in the Allocate equipment group and, where necessary, enter an appropriate value in the associated field.

    The options in the Allocate equipment group on the Equipment Allocation form are used to select:

    • The first item in the range of items displayed

      Select the First item in range option and click the Allocate... button.

    • Other item in the range of items displayed

      The Other item in range option is available only when there is more than one item in the range. Select the Other item in range option, enter the required item's serial number, and click the Allocate... button.

    • An item having a specific serial number

      Select the Specific serial number option, enter the required item's serial number, and click the Allocate... button.

    In each case, the specified item of equipment is allocated.

    Note:

    If equipment allocated to the customer is being reused, a warning message is displayed when the Allocate... button is clicked, prompting confirmation that it should be allocated.

  7. Click the Allocate button. Depending on system configuration, either the Product Instance Detail form or Equipment Reallocation form is displayed.
  8. If the Product Instance Detail form is displayed, click the Save button on the toolbar.
  9. If the Equipment Reallocation form is displayed, enter the appropriate information and click OK.

Changing the Status of an Item of Equipment

To change the status of an item of equipment:

  1. Display the Equipment Detail form for the appropriate equipment item.
  2. Click the Update button on the toolbar. The Equipment Detail form is displayed in Update mode.
  3. Change the setting in the Status field by selecting an alternative option from the drop-down list.

    Note:

    When changing the status of a equipment item to In-Transit, the Transit tab is enabled on the Equipment Detail form. The Transit tab contains details specific to the equipment item while in transit. The appropriate details for the equipment item while in transit are entered on this tab.

  4. Click the Save button on the toolbar.
