Online Help for Singleview Convergent Billing 9.1

Reporting Entity

Convergent Billing records the historical and current information about financial reporting entities for which sales journals are maintained and general ledger (GL) uploads are performed.

Each GL code in Convergent Billing is associated with a reporting entity. The relationship between GL codes and reporting entities allows separate sales journals to be maintained for each reporting entity. A reporting entity acts as a set of books. For example, a multi-national company might maintain one set of books for its US operations, and another for those based in the UK.
