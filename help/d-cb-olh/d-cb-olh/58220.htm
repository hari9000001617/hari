How Do I - Product Configuration Tasks - Product Offers

Select the option most appropriate to the required task.

View a Product Offer

To view a product offer:

  1. Select the Maintenance Products Product Offers option. The Product Offer Search form is displayed.
  2. Enter or select the appropriate search criteria and search method.
  3. Click the Search button. A list of product offers matching the specified criteria is displayed.
  4. Select the appropriate product offer and click the Detail... button, or double-click the appropriate product offer. The Product Offer Definition form is displayed in View Only mode.

Define a Product Offer

Product offers can be created manually, or copied from an existing product offer and modified.

To define a product offer:

  1. Select the Maintenance Products Product Offers option. The Product Offer Search form is displayed.
  2. Click the New... button. The Product Offer Definition form is displayed in Insert mode.
  3. Enter name, display name, description, start date, and if required, end date for the new product offer.
  4. If required, enter a code in the Stock Keeping Unit field, so the product offer can be quickly searched for.
  5. If the product offer is a top-level offer, select the Top-Level Offer? check box.
  6. Select a base product to associate with the offer.
  7. If the product offer is not a top-level offer, and:
    1. If the product offer is to be associated with a specific service, select the Service Specific? check box.
    2. If the product offer is to be hidden when a product is sold to a customer, select the Hidden? check box.
  8. If required, select a category and sub-category for the product offer.
  9. If required, select a customer type to which the product offer is restricted.
  10. Add companion products.
  11. Add child product offers, if required.

    Note:

    A product offer must contain at least one component; that is, either a companion product or child product offer.

  12. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Duplicate a Product Offer

Product offers can be created manually, or copied from an existing product offer and modified.

To duplicate a product offer:

  1. Select the Maintenance Configuration Explorer option. The Configuration Explorer form is displayed.
  2. Click on the Products entry in the left-hand pane of the Configuration Explorer.
  3. Click on the Product Offers entry in the right-hand pane.
  4. Locate the product offer to be duplicated.
  5. Right-click on the product offer, and select Duplicate.... The Duplicate Product Offer form is displayed.
  6. Enter a name for the new product offer.
  7. Click the OK button.
  8. Modify the new product offer as required.

Add a Base Product to a Product Offer

Note:

A base product can be added only to top-level product offers, and only one base product can be added to each product offer.

To add a base product to a product offer:

  1. Modify the appropriate product offer.
  2. Select the Top-Level Offer? check box.
  3. Select the product from the Base Product drop-down list.
  4. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Add a Child Product Offer to a Product Offer

To add a child product offer to a product offer:

  1. View the appropriate product offer and select Update.
  2. Click on the Child Offers tab.
  3. Select one of the rows in the product table, click the Insert button, and select the product offer to be added from the Child Product Offer drop-down list.
  4. Enter the minimum number of optional child product offers in the Offer Min field.

    The child product offer is mandatory if the value is greater than or equal to 1.

    Note:

    If a child product offer is mandatory, it is automatically assigned with the product offer and its availability period is ignored.

    If a child product offer is optional, it is available for sale with the parent offer, if the current date is within the availability period.

  5. Enter the maximum number of optional child product offers in the Offer Max field.

    Offer Max must be greater than or equal to both, Offer Min and 1.

    Note:

    For Offer Max to be 2 or greater, the child product offer must have at least one mandatory companion product. That is, either the minimum number of companion products is greater than or equal to 1, or at least one of the companion products is mandatory.

  6. For optional child product offers, complete the Available Start Date and Available End Date fields as required.
  7. Repeat steps 3 to 6 for any other child product offers to be added.
  8. Enter a minimum and maximum number of child product offers which must be selected when the top-level product offer is sold.
  9. If necessary, rearrange the order of the product offers in the child product offer table using the Up and Down buttons.

    Note:

    The order in which the product offers are listed determines the order in which they are displayed in the tree pane on the Offers tab of the Product Instance Detail form.

  10. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Add a Companion Product to a Product Offer

Note:

To be available for adding to a product offer, a companion product must be added to the compatibility list for the base product associated with the top-level product offer in the hierarchy.

To add a companion product to a product offer:

  1. Edit the appropriate product offer.
  2. Select the Companion Products tab.
    If the base product is associated with a product offer, the Companion Products tab is used to view and change the status of companion product instances selected for this base product instance. Companion product instances are added using the Offer tab and can be created before the product instance has been completed. Any companion instances created on an incomplete product instance receive the initial status of Product Creation.

    If the base product is not associated with a product offer, the Companion Products tab is used to select one or more companion products to be added to this instance of the base product. The available companion products are defined when the product on which this instance is based, is defined. Companion products can be added to this tab only when the product instance is completed.

  3. Select one of the rows in the product table, click the Insert button, select the product to be added to the offer from the Companion Product drop-down list, and select either Yes or No from the Mandatory drop-down list.

    Note:

    If a companion product is configured as mandatory, it is automatically assigned with the product offer.

  4. If the companion product has a derived attribute table associated with it, edit the derived attribute table.

    Note:

    Any derived attribute tables configured in the companion product definition as mandatory must be populated with at least one row.

  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 for other companion products to be added to the product offer.
  6. Enter a minimum and maximum number of companion products that must be selected when the product offer is sold.
  7. If necessary, rearrange the order of the products in the product table using the Up and Down buttons.

    Note:

    The order in which the products are defined determines the order in which those product's icons are displayed when that product group is displayed on the Customer Hierarchy form.

  8. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Modify a Product Offer

A product offer can be updated to:

  • Add or remove optional companion products.
  • Add or remove child product offers.
  • Modify availability dates for the product offer and other attributes of the product offer.
  • Modify derived attributes associated with a companion product, for a date range in the future.
  • The Product Offer Detail view in Business & Administration Studio can be used to perform these updates. It is also possible to view product offers and perform some operations on product offers using the Product Offer Editor in Configuration Studio.

To modify a product offer:

  1. View the appropriate product offer.
  2. Click the Update button on the toolbar. The Product Offer Definition form is displayed in Update mode.
  3. Update the product offer details as required.
  4. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Delete a Product Offer

A product offer cannot be deleted if it is:

  • A child of another product offer.
  • Referenced by a current product instance.

To delete a product offer:

  1. View the appropriate product offer.
  2. Click the Delete button on the toolbar.
  3. When prompted, click the Yes button to confirm that the product offer is to be deleted.

Need more information on product offers?

Associate a Service with a Product Offer

A service can be associated with a product offer if the offer is:

  • Not a top-level offer
  • Configured as a service-specific offer

To associate a service with a product offer:

  1. Display the Product Offer Definition form.
  2. Ensure that the Service Specific? checkbox is selected.
  3. Select a service from the Service drop-down list.
  4. Click the Save button on the toolbar.
