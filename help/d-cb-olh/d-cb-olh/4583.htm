Backend Monitor Process (BMP)

The Backend Monitor Process (BMP) is the monitor of all Convergent Billing processes.

The BMP can be started by the biBmpStart&(), biBmpStartAll&(), biBmpStart&(ProcessNum&), biBmpStart&(InstanceName$), or biBmpStartAlone&() ecp command, normally from within the sv_start utility.

It starts up and monitors the Event Rating Broker (BKR), Task Launch and Monitor (TLM), and Scheduled Task Dispatcher (STD) processes (if their Enabled flags are set to True).

Only one BMP process can exist for each Convergent Billing installation.
