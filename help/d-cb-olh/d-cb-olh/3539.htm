Product Group Definition Form

The Product Group Definition form is used to enter, view, and update a product group.

Base products can be grouped to allow related products to be located more easily when assigning products to customers. Each product group is displayed on a separate 'tab' section on the Customer Hierarchy form.

Used in Customer Management for filtering the Product Catalogue.

Context: Convergent Billing, Customer Management, Configuration Studio

See also Base Product.

The upper part of the form is used to view or enter details about the product group such as its name, description, and effective dates.

The lower part of the form displays a product table, listing products defined as part of the specified group.

Note:

The order in which the products are listed determines the order in which those products’ icons are displayed when the specified product group is displayed.

The form can be displayed in one of three form modes, with the current mode being indicated in the form’s title.

A form can be displayed in one of the following modes:

Associated Actions

Viewing a Product Group

To view a product group:

  1. Select the Maintenance Products Product Group Definition option. The Product Group Definition Search form is displayed.
  2. Enter or select the appropriate search criteria and search method.
  3. Click the Search button. A list of product groups matching the specified criteria is displayed.
  4. Select the appropriate product group and click the Detail... button, or double-click the appropriate product group. The Product Group Definition form is displayed in View Only mode.

Defining a Product Group

To define a product group:

  1. Select the Maintenance Products Product Group Definition option. The Product Group Definition Search form is displayed.
  2. Click the New... button. The Product Group Definition form is displayed in Insert mode.
  3. Enter the name, description, start date and, if required, the end date, for the new product group.
  4. Select the first row in the product table, click the Insert button on the toolbar, and select the product to be added to the group from the drop-down list.
  5. Repeat step 4 for any other products to be added to the product group. If the wrong product is inserted, select the appropriate table row and click the Delete button on the toolbar.
  6. If necessary, rearrange the order of the products in the product table using the Up and Down buttons.

    Note:

    The order in which the products are defined determines the order in which those product's icons are displayed when that product group is displayed on the Customer Hierarchy form.

  7. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Modifying a Product Group

A product group can be modified at any stage after it is defined by:

  • Adding a product to a group.
  • Removing a product that is no longer required.
  • Moving a product to another group.
  • Modifying the group details (for example, name, description, and effective dates).

Need more information on product groups?

Deleting a Product Group

To delete a product group:

  1. View the appropriate product group.
  2. Click the Delete button on the toolbar.
  3. When prompted, click the Yes button to confirm that the product group is to be deleted.
