How Do I - Financial Configuration Tasks - Accounting Years

Select the option most appropriate to the required task.

Define an Accounting Year

To define an accounting year:

  1. Select the Maintenance Accounts Accounting Year option. The Accounting Year Search form is displayed.
  2. Click the New... button. The Accounting Year Definition form is displayed in Insert mode.
  3. In the top part of the form, complete the appropriate data entry fields on the form for the accounting year.
  4. To add accounting periods:
    1. Click in the grid in the middle part of the form.
    2. Click the Insert button.
    3. Complete the appropriate data entry fields in the Accounting Period Details group.

      Note:

      Accounting years and periods defined must conform to the accounting year rules.

      Accounting years and periods, defined using the Accounting Year Definition form, have the following characteristics:

      • No two accounting years belonging to the same accounting calendar can have overlapping date ranges.
      • At least one accounting period must exist for an accounting year.
      • The set of accounting periods for an accounting year must be contiguous; that is, they must completely span the accounting year, with no gaps or overlaps.
      • An accounting period specifies the expected book close date for the period, and the actual book close date.

      Tip:

      An initial set of accounting years and periods can be created using the Generate Accounting Periods schedule type.

  5. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

View an Accounting Year

To view an accounting year:

  1. Select the Maintenance Accounts Accounting Year option. The Accounting Year Search form is displayed.
  2. Enter or select the appropriate search criteria, method, or date.
  3. Click the Search button. A list of accounting years that match the specified criteria is displayed.
  4. Select the appropriate accounting year and click the Detail... button (or double-click the appropriate accounting year). The Accounting Year Definition form is displayed in View Only mode.

Update an Accounting Year

To update an accounting year:

  1. View the appropriate accounting year.
  2. Click the Update button on the toolbar. The Accounting Year Definition form is displayed in Update mode.
  3. In the top part of the form, update the accounting year fields.

    Note:

    The accounting year's status, start date, and end date cannot be updated; they are updated automatically, based on the corresponding fields of the last defined accounting period.

  4. To update accounting periods:
    1. Select an accounting period in the grid in the middle of the form.
    2. Update the fields in the Accounting Period Details group for the selected accounting period.

      Note:

      Accounting years and periods defined must conform to the accounting year rules.

  5. Click the Save button on the toolbar.

Need more information on accounting years?

Delete an Accounting Year

Caution:

If an accounting year is referenced by other entities, if cannot be deleted.

To delete an accounting year:

  1. View the appropriate accounting year.
  2. Click the Delete button on the toolbar.
  3. When prompted, click the Yes button to confirm that the accounting year is to be deleted.
