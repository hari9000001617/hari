Invoice/Statement List Form

The Invoice/Statement List form lists the invoices or statements that match the selection criteria specified on the appropriate search form.

Document itemising charges (and possibly credits) accrued against a customer's account for services used by, or supplied to, that customer over a specified period. Unlike an invoice that itemises charges, a statement provides a record of the charges.

Context: Convergent Billing

See also Invoice and Reporting Level.

Legal document itemising charges (and possibly credits) accrued against that customer's account for services used by, or supplied to, that customer over a specified period. Accrued charges for a customer node in a customer hierarchy can be 'rolled up' into the invoice for a node higher in the customer hierarchy.

Context: Convergent Billing

See also Customer Hierarchy, Customer Node, Reporting Level, and Statement.

To view details of a specific invoice or statement listed on the form, double-click the appropriate entry, or highlight the entry and click the Details… button. The appropriate detail form is displayed.

The display format for the retrieved record(s) can be selected by selecting or clearing the Always Display List? and the Reuse Detail Form? check boxes.

If multiple instances of the detail form are required for display (for example, to access details for several payments) the Reuse detail form? option should not be selected.

Caution:

If selected, the Reuse detail form? option overrides the display of information in a new form; the details are displayed in the existing form.

If a single invoice record is returned from a search, the details of that record are displayed on the Invoice Detail form. If more than one invoice record is returned, the records are listed on the Invoice List form. The required record can then be selected and its details displayed on the Invoice Detail form.

If selected, the Always Display List? option overrides the automatic display of the Invoice Detail form when only a single record is retrieved; in such cases the Invoice List form is displayed.
