Variables Based on INV_OTHER_INVOICES_V

All direct variables based on the INV_OTHER_INVOICES_V view have an application environment of Invoicing and a context of Customer Node.

Variable Definitions

Variable

Type

Description

OtherInvAccountBalance#

Real

Balance of the account after the invoice is generated.

OtherInvAccountId&

Integer

ID of the account to which the invoice is applied.

OtherInvAccountInitialDue#

Real

Initial amount due to be paid on the account.

OtherInvAccountName$

String (30)

Name of the account to which the invoice is applied.

OtherInvBalanceForward#

Real

Balance on the account before the application of the invoice.

OtherInvBillRunId&

Integer

ID the bill run for which the invoice was generated.

OtherInvCurrentDue#

Real

Current amount left to be paid on the invoice.

OtherInvCustAccInvoiceStr$

String (30)

Optional invoice identifier that is generated for the invoice.

OtherInvCustomerInvoiceStr$

String (30)

ID generated for the invoice.

OtherInvCustomerNodeGeneral1$ -
OtherInvCustomerNodeGeneral10$

String (255)

General fields 1 - 10 for storing customer node-specific information.

OtherInvCustomerNodeId&

Integer

ID of the customer node associated with the invoice.

OtherInvCustomerStatus$

String (40)

Status of the customer node. Valid values are:

  • 1 (Prospect)
  • 2 (Credit Check Required)
  • 3 (Active)
  • 4 (Cancelled)
  • 5 (Black-listed).

OtherInvEarlyPaymentAccount
Balance#

Real

Balance of the account at the EARLY_PAYMENT_DATE.

OtherInvEarlyPaymentDate~

Date

If the customer pays before this date, they receive an additional discount.

OtherInvEarlyPaymentDiscount#

Real

Early payment discount that applies for this invoice (if any).

OtherInvEffectiveDate~

Date

Effective date of the bill run that generated the invoice.

OtherInvGeneral1$ -
OtherInvGeneral10$

String (255)

General fields 1 - 10 for invoice or statement specific details.

OtherInvHierarchyLevel&

Integer

Level in the hierarchy of the customer node.

OtherInvInvoiceAmount#

Real

Amount of the invoice.

OtherInvInvoiceId&

Integer

ID of the invoice.

OtherInvInvoiceSeqnr&

Integer

Sequence number that identifies a specific invoice format and image for the customer node.

OtherInvIssueDate~

Date

Date the invoice was issued.

OtherInvNodeName$

String (80)

Name associated with the customer node.

OtherInvoiceSeqnr&

Integer

Sequence number that defines the order the accounts are displayed on the customer's invoice.

OtherInvOriginalPaymentDueDate~

Date

Date when this invoice was originally due to be paid.

OtherInvPaymentDueAccountBalance#

Real

Balance of the account at the PAYMENT_DUE_DATE.

OtherInvPaymentDueDate~

Date

Date when this invoice is due to be paid.

OtherInvPaymentDueDiscount#

Real

Pay on-time discount applied for this invoice (if any).

OtherInvReportGeneral1$ -
OtherInvReportGeneral10$

String (255)

General fields 1 - 10 that can be populated by the invoice hierarchy scripts.

OtherInvReportLevel$

String (40)

Text for the node's reporting level.

OtherInvTotalAdjustments#

Real

Total adjustments made on the account between the issuing of the previous invoice and this invoice.

OtherInvTotalPayments#

Real

Total payments made on the account between the issuing of the previous invoice and this invoice.
