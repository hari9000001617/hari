Integer

EQUIPMENT_
STATUS_CODE

Integer

Equipment status code. Valid values are:

  • 3 (In-Use.)

  • 1 (Provisioning Required)
  • 2 (Provisioned)
  • 5 (Temporary Disconnect Required)
  • 6 (Temporarily Disconnected)
  • 7 (Reactivation Required)
  • 8 (Cancellation Required)
  • 9 (Cancelled)
  • 10 (Reserved)
  • 11 (Repairs Required).
