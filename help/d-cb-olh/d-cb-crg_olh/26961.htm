Product Instance Composite Business Functions

The following table lists the functions available for the CBF interface.

 

Product Instance CBF Interface Functions

Function

Description

fCMN_TREProdInstCompProductAdd_R&()

Adds a companion product to a product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstCompProductCancel_R&()

Cancels a companion product from a product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstCompProductRetrieve_R?[]()

Retrieves the companion products associated with a specified product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstCompProductUpdate_R~()

Updates a companion product for a product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstEquipmentAdd_R&()

Adds and allocates one or more equipment items to a product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstEquipmentCancel_R&()

Cancels an equipment instance from a product instance on a specified date.

fCMN_TREProdInstEquipmentSearch_R?[]()

Searches for product instance equipment matching specified search parameters.

fCMN_TREProdInstEquipmentUpdate_R~()

Updates equipment associated with a product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstFacilityGroupAdd_R&()

Adds a facility group to a specified product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstFacilityGroupCancel_R&()

Cancels a facility group from a specified product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstFacilityGroupRetrieve_R?[]()

Retrieves the facility groups associated with a specified product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstFacilityGroupUpdate_R~()

Updates a facility group instance belonging to a specific product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceAdd_R&()

Adds one or more compatible optional services to a product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceCancel_R&()

Cancels a service belonging to a specified product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceContractAssign_R&()

Assigns a contract to a service belonging to a product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceContractClear_R&()

Detaches a contract from a specific service belonging to a product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceContractCreate_R&()

Creates a contract and associates it with a specific service belonging to a product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceListRowAdd_R&()

Adds a new row to a specified service list.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceListRowDelete_R&()

Deletes a row from a specified service list.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceListRowDeleteMany_R&()

Deletes a row from a specified service list.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceListRowUpdate_R~()

Updates rows in a specified service list.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceRetrieve_R?{}()

Retrieves the details of a service attached to a product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceSearch_R?[]()

Searches for services for a specified product instance.

fCMN_TREProdInstServiceUpdate_R~()

Updates details of the specified service instance.

fCMN_TREProductInstanceCreate_R&()

Creates a product instance and related product instance entities required when selling a product.

fCMN_TREProductInstanceRetrieve_R?{}()

Retrieves the product instance details and related product instance entities.

fCMN_TREProductInstanceSearch_R?[]()

Searches for product instances matching specified search parameters.

fCMN_TREProductInstanceSearchLike_R?[]()

Searches for product instances matching specified search parameters.

fCMN_TREProductInstanceUpdate_R~()

Updates the details of a specified product instance.
