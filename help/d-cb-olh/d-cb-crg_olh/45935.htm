INV_CONSOLIDATED_STATEMENTS_V

The INV_CONSOLIDATED_STATEMENTS_V view defines statements from the current bill run, or a previous bill run, that are consolidated into a consolidated invoice for the current bill run.

The information is similar to that in the INV_INVOICE_V view, but applies to consolidated statements. Details not applicable to statements are not included in this view.

This view allows retrieval of statement information using the default WHERE clause of the other invoices template type. The following default WHERE clauses are used to retrieve statements associated with a consolidated invoice:

  • prime_customer_node_id=:1 AND
    bill_run_id=:2 AND
    invoice_seqnr=:3
  • consolidation_invoice_id=:1 AND
    invoice_seqnr=:2
